After Care

After care is very important to make sure your tattoo heals correctly.
Remove the bandage

After 2-4 hours, remove your bandage and wash gentle with warm water and antibacterial soap.
Blot dry with a disposable paper towel. Do not use the first or last paper towel in the roll.

Washing

Make sure to gently wash your tattoo with warm water and antibacterial soap.
Blot tattoo try with dispoable paper towel. Do not use the first or last paper towel in the roll.

Keep your tattoo moisturized!

When your tattoo is healing, it is important to keep it moisturized.
Apply a small ammount of vitamin A&D ointment or Palmer's Cocoa Butter Lotion
3-4 times a day. Make sure to wash your hands with antibacterial soap before
making contant with the tattoo. Becareful to not over moisturize!

Leave it alone!

During the healing process of your tattoo, it will start to get itchy and peel.
This is totally normal and all part of the healing process! Peeling or scratching
at your tattoo can effect your healed tattoo. Make sure to not soak your tattoo,
avoid pools, hot tubs, and long baths.

Sunlight!

Sunlight is one of the worst things for your healing tattoo! Make sure to try and
stay out of direct sunlight as much as possible for the first 2 weeks. Always
make sure to wear HIGH SPF sunblock on your tattoo, even after it is healed!
Keeping these bring demoned rays from your tattoo will allow it to stay bold
and beautfiul for a long time!
